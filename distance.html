Change in Race Distance

In this graph we can observe the total distance of the Tour over the years. The race was notorious in its conception and was more a joke than anything. Though, as the race gained popularity we can observe that more of a focus was made. By that I mean that the race included more challegnes such as harsh climbs and other variable that would challegne the riders while still maintaining the concept of a race. The initial blue line idicates a period of regulation changes, the second for WWI and the final for WWII.

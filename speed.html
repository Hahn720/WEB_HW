Average Speed v. Winning Times

This graph displays the average speed of the riders vs the overall completion time of the race. With direct correlation to distance, the time in which the race is completed has also gone down, though with full credit to the riders as most would say the difficulty of the race has increased over time. The initial blue line idicates a period of regulation changes, the second for WWI and the final for WWII.
